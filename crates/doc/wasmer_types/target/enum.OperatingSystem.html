Darwin(Option<DeploymentTarget>)

The general Darwin core OS.

Generally, -mmacosx-version-min=... or similar flags are required by Clang to determine the actual OS (either macOS, iOS, tvOS, watchOS or visionOS).

WARNING: When parsing rustc target triples, this matches the macOS target triples as well.
